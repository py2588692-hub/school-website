Types and Levels of Schooling
Most modern education systems divide schooling into sequential stages:
Early Childhood/Pre-school: Focuses on early learning and developmental foundations for young children.
Primary/Elementary School: Typically covers grades 1 through 5 or 8, providing basic literacy and numeracy.
Secondary/High School: Divided into lower and upper secondary (often ending at Grade 12), preparing students for university or vocational life.,br> Higher Education:
Includes colleges and universities where students earn degrees in specific disciplines.
